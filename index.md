# Iowa Community Blueprints
### Strong, independent support networks right on our blocks—without red tape, complex laws, or corporate tracking.

[View Open-Source Data Assets (Master Repository)](https://github.com/mmonfils/iowa-community-blueprints)

***

## Our Core Belief
Every single person living on a block has full standing, absolute dignity, and an immediate right to be included. Your place in this neighborhood is never decided by property ownership, income, or background. We use plain, action-oriented language to focus entirely on practical results: block safety, tool sharing, and protecting our neighbors.

## Explore the Community Tracks

* **[Social Infrastructure Track](./social-track/)** Field manuals for building resilient neighbor pods, establishing local text communication loops, and organizing crisis absorption networks.
  
* **[Economic Solidarity Track](./economic-track/)** Anonymized, offline spreadsheets to help neighbors catalog heavy tools, swap skills, and log mutual help without using money.
  
* **[Policy & Political Track](./policy-political-track/)** Plain-language toolkits to help everyday people deconstruct city codes, monitor corporate property networks, and build bottom-up fiscal arguments.
  
* **[Independent Investigations Workspace](./investigations/)** An open evidence vault tracking regional administrative gaps and operational bottlenecks—fully anchored back to public records with absolute integrity.
