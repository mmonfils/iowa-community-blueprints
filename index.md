# Iowa Community Blueprints
### Strong, independent support networks right on our streets and rural roads, free from red tape, complex laws, or corporate influence.

[View Open Public Ledger (Main Repository)](https://github.com/mmonfils/iowa-community-blueprints)

***

## Our Core Belief
Every single person living in our local areas has full standing, absolute dignity, and an immediate right to be included. Your place in the neighborhood is never decided by property ownership, income, or background. We use plain, action-oriented language to focus entirely on practical results, including community safety, tool sharing, and protecting our neighbors.

## Explore the Community Guides

* **[Social Infrastructure Track](./social-track/)** Field manuals for building resilient neighbor groups, establishing local communication loops, and organizing support networks.
  
* **[Economic Solidarity Track](./economic-track/)** Anonymized, offline templates to help neighbors catalog tools, swap skills, and log mutual help without using money.
  
* **[Policy & Political Track](./policy-political-track/)** Plain-language toolkits to help everyday people understand regional codes, monitor corporate property networks, and build bottom-up fiscal arguments.
  
* **[Independent Investigations Workspace](./investigations/)** An open public archive tracking regional administrative gaps and operational bottlenecks, fully anchored back to public records with absolute integrity.
