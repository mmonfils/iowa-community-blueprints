# LEGISLATIVE AMENDMENT DRAFT: REGIONAL CRISIS STABILIZATION RISK POOLS

**Subject:** Statutory Amendment Text to Unblock House File 385 Allocation Stalemate  
**Target Statutory Placement:** Iowa Code Chapter 225C (Mental Health and Disability Services)  

### Section 1. Intent and Establishment
The purpose of this amendment is to insulate local hospital networks from uncompensated care liabilities occurring during the statutory fifteen (15) day transitional discharge period mandated under House File 385. There is hereby established within each regional Administrative Services Organization (ASO) an Iowa Regional Crisis Stabilization Risk Pool.

### Section 2. Operational Mechanics & Fund Routing
1. **Risk Pool Caps:** Each regional ASO shall allocate a designated portion of its baseline mental health and disability services fund to serve as a backstop for uncompensated transitional pharmaceutical claims.
2. **Eligibility Routing:** When an involuntarily committed individual is discharged and lacks immediate active Medicaid coverage, commercial health insurance, or third-party pharmacy benefits, the dispensing regional facility or contracted pharmacy network shall route the uncompensated transitional invoice directly to the regional ASO Risk Pool ledger.
3. **Reimbursement Rate:** Reimbursements from the Risk Pool shall be paid at the established state Medicaid fee-for-service acquisition rate for psychotropic medications, preventing cost-inflation overhead.
