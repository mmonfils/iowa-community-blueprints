# Policy Navigator Track: Understanding Local Rules and Protecting Our Space

Welcome to the policy navigator track. This directory provides simple, plain-language templates to help everyday people analyze local laws, track corporate property groups, and build clear, numbers-backed arguments to protect nearby homes and land without needing expensive legal teams or complex political networks.

## Core Operational Objectives

* **Demystifying Local Codes:** Break down complicated zoning laws, city rules, and regional guidelines into simple, easy-to-use compliance checklists.
* **Tracking Local Shifts:** Keep a close watch on large corporate developers, out-of-state property groups, or sudden municipal planning changes that threaten the safety and peace of our immediate area.
* **Building Independent Briefings:** Equip everyday neighbors with authoritative, easy-to-read guides to show public supervisors exactly how administrative roadblocks hurt local families.
* **Preserving Information Autonomy:** Ensure all local research is conducted and saved on free, open platforms owned directly by the community.

## Folder Contents

* **[Big Picture Framework](./big-picture-framework.md)** A basic structural template to analyze major policy adjustments, town planning developments, or statutory changes to map out immediate regional impact.
* **[SBAR Briefing Template](./sbar-briefing-template.md)** A clear, four-quadrant structured format (Situation, Background, Assessment, Recommendation) designed to establish an authoritative briefing once a specific local friction point is identified.
* **[Action Roadmap Template](./action-roadmap-template.md)** A compact timeline guide engineered to map out localized execution phases, evidence-gathering milestones, and administrative rollout dates across a four-quarter horizon.
* **[Peer Review Examples](./peer-review-examples.md)** A reference chart containing data-verified domestic and global peer-community models used to ground local solutions with concrete, real-world alternatives.

## Privacy Mandate

To maintain strict privacy and data integrity, documents within this folder must exclusively record public entities, official administrative departments, corporate actors, or public officials. Storing private citizen identities, unlisted personal contact information, or sensitive case notes regarding specific local families on this public server is strictly prohibited.
