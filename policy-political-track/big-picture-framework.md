# Big Picture Framework

Use this template to sort through outside developments, policy shifts, or official updates so the block can look past the noise and figure out what it actually means for our neighborhood.

---

## 1. The Big Picture Challenge
*   **What change or ongoing development are we looking at?** (Such as zoning updates, changes to commuter routes, outside expansion of large organizations).
*   **Where is this coming from?** (Such as city hall, outside development groups, regional planning authorities).
*   **What is the official timeline or target date?** 

---

## 2. The Pitch and the Messaging
*   **What is the official story or angle being used?** (List the specific buzzwords, slogans, or promotional talking points being shared in official notices or media).
*   **What problem is this change meant to solve?**
*   **What details are missing?** (Note the gaps between official promises and the everyday reality of our neighborhoods).

---

## 3. The Action Plan for Our Blocks
*   **How does this directly impact our ability to take care of our own neighborhood?** (Consider things like: Will this reshape our shared public resources, affect neighbor privacy, or change who controls the immediate space?).
*   **What are the early signs to look out for on our streets?** 
*   **What simple, proactive steps can everyday neighbors take to strengthen their block?**
