# Front-Porch Communication Standards

Our group operates entirely on horizontal, block-level mutual accountability. We enforce an unyielding baseline rule for all organizing spaces, message threads, and physical assemblies:

1. **Universal Human Dignity Standard:** Every single person living on a block has full standing, absolute dignity, and an immediate right to be included. Standing is never dictated by homeownership, rent status, income level, background, or duration of residency.
2. **Radial De-Polarization:** Partisan political labeling, ideological talking points, and elite academic buzzwords are strictly banned from our organizing targets. We speak exclusively in the universal language of block stability, shared assets, and collective protection.
3. **Accountability:** We work together to address safety, housing, or resource friction locally using mutual aid before involving top-down legacy municipal agencies or enforcement nodes.
