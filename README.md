# Iowa Community Blueprints

### Live Mobile Landing Page: [https://mmonfils.github.io/iowa-community-blueprints/](https://mmonfils.github.io/iowa-community-blueprints/)
*(Note: For the best experience on a mobile phone web browser, use the link above to view our polished, easy-to-read web layout.)*

This project is an open-source technical repository providing policy tracking frameworks, structural toolkits, and routine coordination tools to reinforce community safety and civil well-being in Iowa. It is a practical, community-run framework engineered to help everyday people coordinate resources, protect local infrastructure, and build resilient support networks right where they live.

We believe that every person has equal standing and an absolute right to be included. You do not need to own a home, have a special title, or deal with red tape to make your neighborhood a better, safer place.

***

## How the Repository is Set Up

To keep things organized and easy to navigate on a phone screen, our tools are split into four main tracks. Click any link below to jump directly into that folder:

* **[Social Infrastructure Track](./social-infrastructure/)** Practical toolkits for building resilient neighbor pods, establishing local communication channels, and organizing crisis support networks across nearby homes.
* **[Economic Solidarity Track](./economic-solidarity/)** Offline spreadsheets to help neighbors share tools, swap skills, and trade everyday help without using money.
* **[Policy Navigator Track](./policy-navigator/)** Plain-language templates to help neighbors understand local city rules, protect their space, and track outside pressures like predatory developers.
* **[Investigations Workspace](./investigations/)** Rigorous, system-level research briefs and analyses tracking systemic gaps and policy-driven local friction.
